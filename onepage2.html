Who we are:

Teens Armor is a company committed to empowerment and equipping of the teenagers and youths to meet the demands of the 21st century through coaching programs.

Programs

  • pd

    A fun, engaging and positively challenging program that equips teens to discover and master personal strengths and nurture the skills of assertiveness, excellence, self-drive and initiative, creativity, problem solving, people skills and self-expression.

  • Kloza

    Ever desired to connect with teens better, ever desired to nurture your skills to engage them in a more productive way? Kloza offers you insights and empowers you to nurture skills to do so. Ideal for parents, churches, schools and social workers.

  • TeensTech

    The future belongs to mathematics and science. We influence positive academic performance in mathematics and sciences in secondary schools and exposure to the marketplace tech in alignment with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

  • The liberated soul

    Through worship experience and empowerment talks we support teenagers and youths to heal emotional wounds to fully express themselves as a gifts to the universe and to connect deeper with self and the world in a more meaningful and fulfilling way.

  • Unstuck

    Do you have a pressing issue that you feel is holding you back and you just need someone to help you rise above it? We give you a platform to express your worries without judgment or manipulation. Our team is skilled to specifically connect and inspire teenagers. We therefore bring to the table much understanding of your world and trust building skills.
